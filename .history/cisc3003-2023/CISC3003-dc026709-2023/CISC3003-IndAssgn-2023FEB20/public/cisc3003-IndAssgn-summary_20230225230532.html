My Goal and Intended Learning Outcomes

Web development is a field that combines creativity, logic and problem-solving. As a web developer, I want to create websites that are functional, user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing. My goal is to work as a freelance web developer and offer my services to clients who need websites for their businesses or personal projects.

This goal is important to me because it aligns with my values, interests and passions. I value freedom, flexibility and autonomy in my work. I enjoy learning new things, experimenting with different technologies and expressing my ideas through code. I am passionate about making the web more accessible, inclusive and engaging for everyone.

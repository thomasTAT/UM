My education

The programme covers the main aspects of modern computer science and related technologies. It also provides students with opportunities to participate in research projects and internships.

CISC3014 Information Retrieval and Web Search

I took CISC3014 Information Retrieval and Web Search, where I learned the basics of Python. During the course, I utilized Python and TF-IDF to calculate document similarity.
